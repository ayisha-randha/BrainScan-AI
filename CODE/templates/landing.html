Platform Capabilities

Clinical AI Meets
Hospital Management

Every stage of the diagnostic journey — from patient registration to final PDF report — handled in one unified platform.

Dual AI Classification

CNN extracts visual features — edges, textures, abnormal tissue. EfficientNet-B0 applies transfer learning for state-of-the-art accuracy across 4 tumor classes.

Grad-CAM Heatmaps

Visual saliency maps highlight the exact MRI regions that influenced each prediction — making every AI decision interpretable and clinically trustworthy.

End-to-End Workflow

Full appointment lifecycle: booking → consultation → MRI request → lab scheduling → AI scan → doctor review → prescription → patient report.

Lab MRI Module

Dedicated lab portal for receiving scan requests, scheduling MRI appointments, uploading images, and automatically triggering AI analysis upon upload.

Automated PDF Reports

Professionally formatted reports include the original MRI scan, Grad-CAM overlay, AI prediction with confidence, doctor notes, and prescribed treatment.

Real-time Notifications

Every role receives instant in-app alerts — appointment confirmations, scan uploads, low-confidence flags, diagnosis updates, and prescription notifications.

Clinical Workflow

From Symptom to
Confirmed Diagnosis

1
Patient Registers & Books

Patient creates an account, selects a specialist, chooses a date and time slot, and submits symptoms — all online.

2
Doctor Consultation

Doctor reviews patient history, evaluates symptoms, and decides: prescribe directly or request an MRI from the lab.

3
Lab Schedules & Scans

Lab staff receive the MRI request, schedule the scan, perform the procedure, and upload the image to the system.

4
AI Analysis (CNN + EfficientNet-B0)

Deep learning models classify the tumor type in under 5 seconds. Confidence scores and Grad-CAM heatmaps are generated automatically.

5
Doctor Reviews & Prescribes

Doctor validates the AI prediction, adds clinical notes, confirms the diagnosis, and prescribes treatment or follow-up care.

6
Patient Downloads Report

Patient receives a real-time notification and securely downloads a comprehensive PDF report including scan, heatmap, diagnosis, and prescription.
